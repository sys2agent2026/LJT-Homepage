<h1 id="about-me">About Me</h1>

<p>I am <strong>Junteng Liu</strong>, a first-year PhD candidate at the <strong>HKUST NLP Group</strong>, supervised by Professor Junxian He. I graduated from Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU) in June 2024 with a B.Eng. degree.</p>

<p>My research focuses on <strong>natural language processing</strong> and <strong>machine learning</strong>. I am particularly interested in <em>LLM Reasoning and Reinforcement Learning</em>, <em>Hallucination in Vision-Language Models (VLM)</em>, and <em>LLM Truthfulness and Interpretability</em>.</p>
