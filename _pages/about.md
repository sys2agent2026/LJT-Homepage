  <h1 id="about-me">About Me</h1>

  <p class="lead">I am <strong>Junteng Liu</strong>, a first-year PhD candidate at the <a href="https://www.cs.ust.hk/~jxhe/hkust-nlp/">HKUST NLP Group</a>, supervised by Professor <a href="https://www.cs.ust.hk/~jxhe/">Junxian He</a>. My research focuses on <strong>natural language processing</strong> and <strong>machine learning</strong>, with particular interests in <em>LLM Reasoning and Reinforcement Learning</em>, <em>Hallucination in Vision-Language Models</em>, and <em>LLM truthfulness and Interpretability</em>.</p>
